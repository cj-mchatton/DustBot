DustBot Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Privacy in plain language

DustBot does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Local game data

Game progress, stars, coins, streaks, and settings are stored only in DustBot's private app container on your device. This data is not sent to us or to third parties. Removing the app may remove this local data.

Accounts, advertising, and analytics

DustBot has no user accounts, advertising, in-app purchases, analytics service, tracking technology, or required network connection.

Children

Because DustBot collects no personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If a future version changes its data practices, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before that version is released.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer using the support address listed on DustBot's App Store page.
