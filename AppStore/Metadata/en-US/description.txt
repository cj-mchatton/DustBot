Tiny bot. Big mess. Impeccable standards.

DustBot is a cozy route-drawing puzzle game about planning the perfect clean. Touch DustBot, draw one continuous path through every crumb, avoid household hazards, and return safely to the charging dock.

FEATURES

• Draw routes with simple one-finger controls
• Play turn-based Cat Chase rooms with one-tile swipes
• Outsmart a predictable two-move pursuer using furniture and corners
• Clean 6,000 deterministic Main Journey rooms
• Learn naturally through 15 handcrafted tutorial puzzles
• Take on a fresh Daily Challenge and build your streak
• Unlock demanding Master Clean rooms
• Keep going in persistent Endless Clean mode
• Earn up to three stars for efficient, independent solutions
• Use coins earned through play when you want a hint
• Enjoy original procedural music, playful sound effects, and tactile animation
• Play completely offline

NO FUSS, JUST PUZZLES

DustBot contains no ads, no in-app purchases, no accounts, no tracking, and no required internet connection. Your progress stays on your device.
