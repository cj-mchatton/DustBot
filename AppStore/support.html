DustBot Support

How do I draw a route?

Touch DustBot, then drag through neighboring tiles. Drag backward onto the previous tile to erase. Clean every crumb and finish at the dock before pressing Play.

How do hints work?

Hints cost 10 Dust Coins. Coins are earned by completing rooms and daily challenges. DustBot has no purchases or advertising.

How do I restore or reset progress?

Progress is stored locally on your device. To reset it, open Settings and tap Reset Progress twice to confirm. DustBot does not use cloud accounts.

Need more help?

Contact the developer using the support email listed on DustBot's App Store page and include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
