# Age rating guidance

Answer **None** or **No** for all content descriptors and capabilities:

- no violence;
- no profanity or crude humor;
- no mature, sexual, medical, drug, alcohol, or tobacco content;
- no horror or fear themes;
- no gambling, contests, loot boxes, or chance-based purchases;
- no unrestricted web access;
- no user-generated content, messaging, or social features;
- no advertising;
- no in-app purchases.

The expected global rating is the lowest general-audience tier, subject to App Store Connect's current questionnaire and regional rules.
